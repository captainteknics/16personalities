Survival Skills Information

Due: Worksheet is due Class 3 by 11:59 p.m. See Instructional Plan for exact due dates. The presentations will take place during Class 4.

Submit to: The Survival Skills Worksheet submission folder. Submission folders can be accessed by selecting Course Tools and then Assignments on the course navigation bar.

Value: 10% of the final grade (the worksheet accounts toward 4 points and the presentation accounts for 20 points).

Note

You must submit your survival skills worksheet beforehand or you will not be permitted to present your presentation. You will receive a 0% on the presentation if you do not submit your worksheet.

Description

For the Survival Skills Presentation, you will be creating a presentation on how to do a specific task. The presentation should teach the audience how to complete an essential task in their day-to-day life, such as how to make coffee.

Rationale

This assignment will evaluate the following course learning outcomes:

2.2 – Prepare presentations as an individual, as well as collaborate with peers to prepare & deliver an effective presentation as a team.

2.5 - Research & plan content to achieve process driven effectiveness.

2.6 – Demonstrate time management in planning & delivery.

2.7 – Design & rehearse structure of presentation (introduction, content, conclusion).

Directions

Select each item to learn more.

Step 1: Choose a Partner or Group of Three

You can only work in pairs or groups of three. You are unable to work individually.

Step 2: Survival Skills Worksheet

Complete the survival skills worksheet, specifying who your partner is, the objective of the presentation, and an outline of the presentation. You should also list four references that you plan to use, two of which must be academic articles.

The worksheet should be submitted by the end day of Class 3.

Note

Each pair should submit once. Please ensure you are both registered in the same group number, otherwise only the individual who submitted the assignment will receive a grade.

Step 3: Survival Skills Presentation

Please ensure your presentation meets the following criteria.

Time: 5-7 minutes

The time must be equally shared between the presenters.

Topics: Pairs can choose their own topics but must gain approval from the professor prior to developing the presentation.

Visual Aids: You may use PowerPoint or other visual aids such as pictures.

You can also use props to support your presentation.

All visual aids should support your presentation BUT should not be the presentation.

Include an APA title page & reference list.

Research: The presentation must be supported by credible and appropriate research.

  • You must have a total of 4 references from the Conestoga Library Database
  • You must have a minimum of 2 academic, peer reviewed journal articles.
  • You may use other credible information sources such as newspapers and websites.

The presentation will take place in Class 4. You must also submit your visual aids by the beginning of Class 4. This should be submitted through email.

Note

Each pair should submit once. Please ensure you are both registered in the same group number, otherwise only the individual who submitted the assignment will receive a grade.
