Assignment: Verbal Demonstration 1 Information

Due: Class 2 during class. See Instructional Plan for exact due dates.

Value: 5% of the final grade.

Description

This verbal demonstration is an opportunity to gain a better understanding of your personality and reflect on how it may affect your presentation style. For this presentation you will determine and develop strategies to build on your strengths and develop your weaknesses.

Rationale

This assignment will evaluate the following course learning outcomes:

2.2 – Prepare presentations as an individual, as well as collaborate with peers to prepare & deliver an effective presentation as a team.

2.6 – Demonstrate time management in planning & delivery.

2.7 – Design & rehearse structure of presentation (introduction, content, conclusion).

Directions

Select each item to learn more.

Part 1: Take the Personality Test

  1. Go to the site: 16Personalities
  2. Click the “Take the Test” button
  3. Complete the test honestly
  4. Read your results

Part 2: Reflect on your results

  • Do you think they are accurate?
  • What strengths do you have that could help you to communicate and carry out presentations more effectively?
  • What challenges may you face based on your personality?
  • How can you overcome these challenges?

Part 3: Put together a short presentation

  • 4-5 minutes long
  • 4 slides/visual aids
  • Include the following information:
    • Brief outline of the results
    • Do you agree with them?
    • Your strengths + challenges
    • Strategies for the future
    • Something interesting about yourself

Part 4: Present in Class
